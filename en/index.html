enjoy your next vacation in Katashina

Oze Iwakura Highlands is rich in nature and is full of various sports and leisure activities throughout the year. At Lodge Nonaka, we offer great value accommodation plans for each season.

5th Floor - Bath

The bathroom on the top floor houses a spacious bath with great panoramic views of the slopes. You can enjoy a relaxing bath while seeing the vast nature of the slopes in summer, and the pure white snow scenery in winter. The bathroom is open 24 hours a day. The bathroom also has a sauna for you to enjoy.

3rd & 4th Floors - Guest Rooms

The guest rooms are Japanese-style rooms with a relaxing atmosphere. All rooms are equipped with a toilet, washbasin, TV, refrigerator, and closet.

2nd Floor - Restaurant

From the window of the second floor restaurant, you can enjoy both excellent food and looking out at the wonderful, vast slopes of White World Oze Iwakura.
