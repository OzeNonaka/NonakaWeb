Facilities

"Lodge Nonaka" is located on the slopes of White World Oze Iwakura, the largest of the Katashina ski resorts. A great location with the vast slopes right in front of you! The 5-story building is the tallest in the area, and you can see the slopes from the windows of your room.

  • 2nd Floor - From the windows of the restaurant on the 2nd floor, you can enjoy a wonderful evening while looking out at the vast slopes of White World Oze Iwakura.
    •  
  • 3rd and 4th Floors - The guest rooms are Japanese-style rooms with a relaxing atmosphere.
    All rooms are equipped with a toilet, washbasin, TV, refrigerator, and closet. 13 guest rooms, accommodating 70 people.
    •  
  • 5th Floor - The bathroom on the top floor houses a spacious bath with great panoramic views of the slopes.
    Enjoy a relaxing time while looking at the vast nature of the slopes in summer and the pure white snow scenery in winter. Bathing is available 24 hours a day, and there is also a sauna in the bathroom.
